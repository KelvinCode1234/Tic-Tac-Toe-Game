Tic -Tac- Toe

How to Play

Click on an empty square to place your symbol ( X or O ).

Take turns with your opponent (or play solo), be patient.

Win the game by getting three in a row,either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

If the board is full and no one has won, then it's a draw.

Hint

Pay attention to the AI's moves to anticipate its strategy.

Use blocking tactics to prevent the AI from winning.

Think ahead and plan your moves carefully to outsmart the AI!
